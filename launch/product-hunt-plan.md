# Product Hunt Launch Plan (Tuesday/Wednesday)

## Recommended timing
- Launch day: Tuesday or Wednesday
- Go live: 12:01 AM PT
- Keep 6-8 hours blocked for active replies/comments

## Assets to prepare
- Tagline (<= 60 chars)
- Short description
- First comment (your build story + roadmap)
- Gallery: 5 images + 1 short demo video
- Maker profile fully filled
- CTA link (GitHub or landing page)

## Launch week checklist
- T-3 days: finalize screenshots/video + listing text
- T-2 days: soft share to friends/community for feedback
- T-1 day: final QA and schedule launch assets
- Launch day: post, reply to all comments, share across LinkedIn/X
- +24h: publish recap with metrics and learnings

## Cross-post sequence on launch day
1. Product Hunt post live
2. LinkedIn post (15-30 min after)
3. Twitter/X thread (30-60 min after)
4. Show HN post (later same day with technical detail)

## Metrics to track
- Upvotes
- Comments
- Demo views
- Store installs
